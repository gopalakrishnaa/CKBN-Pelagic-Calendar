About Our Pelagic Trips

Join us for an exciting birding adventure on the Arabian Sea off Mangaluru coast. We offer three types of trips:

  • Half Day Trip (6AM-1PM): Perfect for beginners or those with limited time.
  • Full Day Trip (6AM-6PM): Explore deeper waters and see more pelagic species.
  • Overnight Trip (6AM-Next Day 6PM): The ultimate pelagic experience with opportunities to observe nocturnal seabirds.

Regular trips are conducted on weekends only. Special weekday trips may be arranged upon request.
